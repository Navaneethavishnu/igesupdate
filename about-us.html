About us

As part of its larger mandate to develop human potential to its fullest, the IIT Delhi has launched an Initiative on Gender Equity and Sensitisation (IGES), on International Women’s Day - March 8, 2018. IGES aims to create and sustain a safe, inclusive and violence-free educational atmosphere in the Institute, which fosters growth and solidarity irrespective of sex, gender, caste, class, ethnicity, language, race, dis/ability and sexual orientation.

IGES builds on the work conducted by Gender Awareness Committee instituted in IIT in 2015. The GAC has been conduction gender sensitisation workshops and awareness on sexual harassment at workplace sessions with students and staff.

The IGES unit looks forward to your contribution in shaping this new body by taking initiative and interest around gender and diversity issues. The Institute has zero tolerance against sexual harassment, and a comprehensive policy has been formulated to confidentially act on any complaints.
