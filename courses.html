Building Capacities

Strengthening the capacities of people, organizations, and communities ensures sustainable and enduring change. We play our part by engaging stakeholders, facilitating training, managing learning exchanges and developing learning materials that outlast the lifetime of our projects. ASSIST develops knowledge and skills to address the root issues of problems thereby, ensuring lasting results.

Highlights from 2018

IGES is the nodal body for building capacities and generating awareness on gender in the campus with different departments and units. We do short sessions, organise talks with experts, film screenings and workshops on gender, sexuality, sexual harassment at workplace and other related issues..

♠♠♠♠ Reached out to 3500 people from the IIT Delhi community through sensitisation events, workshops, need assessment and stakeholder meetings. ♠♠♠♠

Workshops

Workshops on Gender and Sexual Harassment in 2018-19

♠ 10 workshops with 591 PhD students from the departments of Electrical Eng., Physics, Centre for Energy Studies and Chemical Eng., were conducted in November and December 2018.
♠ 2 workshops with 79 faculty members from the departments of Electrical Eng., Design, Material Sciences & Development, ITMMEC, Textile Technology were conducted in November 2018 and February 2019.
♠ 10 workshops with 266 personnel of the Security Unit, covering 254 guards and supervisors, and 12 staff were conducted in October and November 2018.
1 workshop with 54 library and hospital staff members was conducted in February 2019.

Strengthening Capacity

Strengthening Capacity of Institute's Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)

IGES has held meetings with the internal members of ICC to work out ways to strengthen its capacities as well as build awareness around the institute's zero tolerance policy towards sexual harassment.
